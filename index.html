About Me

I am a software engineer at Google Mountain View, and I work on security/cryptographic systems for user identity and authentication.

Before that, I received my PhD from the University of Waterloo in January 2015. My research interests include analysis and design of symmetric-key based cryptographic algorithms and protocols.
